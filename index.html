Good to Know

A few common questions

Do you deliver?

No, we're collection only — ring ahead and we'll have your order ready to pick up.

Do you take card payments?

We're cash only at the moment, so please bring cash when collecting your order.

Can you leave out MSG or specific allergens?

Just let us know when you order and we'll do our best to accommodate. See the allergen key on our menu page for details on each dish.

How long will my order take?

Collection orders are usually ready in 20–30 minutes, though it can vary at busy times — we'll give you an estimate when you call.
